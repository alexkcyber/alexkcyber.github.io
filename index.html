Cybersecurity

Not only is cybersecurity my major, but it is also my passion. As technology advances and computers become more advanced, the importance for secure systems becomes greater. This is an everlasting challenge, though. Systems become more complex, and as a result, the attack surfaces broaden. I have dedicated much of my time and research towards the security domain, as it is an utmost importance to keep up with the continuous threat of new attacks as software and hardware become more complex.
